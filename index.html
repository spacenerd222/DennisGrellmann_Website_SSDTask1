What is a computer?

A computer is an electronic device that can perform a wide range of operations, including inputting, processing, storing, and outputting data. It operates using a combination of hardware and software components, and can perform tasks such as word processing, browsing the internet, playing games, and much more. Computers are essential tools for communication, work, entertainment, and education, and have become an integral part of modern life.

Where computers are found today!

  • Your phone, its actually a small computer!
  • Cars, most modern cars have inbuilt computers!
  • Planes, they have computers for flight and navigation!
  • Smart Watches, they are mini computers that you wear everyday.
